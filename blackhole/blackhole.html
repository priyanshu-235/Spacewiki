Black Holes

  • Sagittarius A*
  • M87 Black Hole
  • Cygnus X-1

Sagittarius A*

Sagittarius A* is the supermassive black hole located at the center of the Milky Way galaxy. It has a mass approximately 4 million times that of the Sun and is surrounded by a dense cluster of stars.

M87 Black Hole

The M87 black hole is one of the largest known black holes, located in the Virgo galaxy cluster. It gained fame as the first black hole to be directly imaged in 2019 by the Event Horizon Telescope.

Cygnus X-1

Cygnus X-1 is a stellar-mass black hole in the Milky Way galaxy. It was one of the first black hole candidates discovered and has a mass approximately 21 times that of the Sun.
