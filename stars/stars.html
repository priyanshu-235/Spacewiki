Sirius

Sirius, also known as the Dog Star, is the brightest star in Earth's night sky. It is part of the constellation Canis Major and is a binary star system.

Vega

Vega is the brightest star in the constellation Lyra and was once the northern pole star. It is a relatively young and hot star.

Polaris

Polaris, also known as the North Star, is located nearly directly above the North Pole and has been used for navigation for centuries.
